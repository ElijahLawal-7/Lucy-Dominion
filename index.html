Our Vision

A society where every girl-child is seen as an integral member of the society and is given a level ground to attaining her full potentials required to transforming her world!

Our Mission

• To Promote the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals 3,4,&5 indicators as related to women and children
• To inspire the vulnerable women and children discover purpose, unleash and maximize their potentials.

Our Community

Our Community is made up of passionate Volunteers, Social Impact workers, skilled professionals who are actively advancing the UNSDGs, and striving relentlessly to make the world a better place, particularly for the girl-child, and children.

Our Programmes

Lucy-Dominion Foundation runs several programs which includes but not limited to;
Outreaches such as medical or skill development outreaches; Scholarship; Mentorship and Leadership Trainings; Special Events and Workshops;
Scholarships; Health Education; Advocacy and campaigns; Schools/orphanage outreaches and More;

Our Programmes

Outreaches

Our outreaches to schools have over the years proven effective in carrying out several intervention campaigns. It provides Window to reach out to both the young girls and their male counterparts in the bid to addressing some of these challenges encountered by them. Our friendly and effective models helps to unmask the most concealed challenges of women and children, and to provide solutions to them.

Entrepreneurial Skills Development

To economically empower and combat several forms of inequalities encountered by these vulnerable girls and young women, relevant skills for self-reliance and sufficiency are being taught and provision of equipment such as grinding machines, sewing machines, hair dryer, baking equipment etc shall equally be provided! Vocational centers are underway.

Scholarship/Mentorship

To promote child education, we provide scholarship opportunities such as provision of educational materials, school fees and exam fees such as NECO, WAEC for schoolchildren, especially vulnerable girls!. Also, trainings aimed at capacity building of our personnel, members and volunteers and Peer education trainings in selected schools are part of our usual and conventional activities and programs.
